ABOUT

Rate Your Dorms was conceptualized in January of 2017 by two first year students. While in a study room of Ehinghaus dorm, the two students discussed the various needs of other first year students. The most common complaint of our peers was the first year living situation and the lack of information. It is now our mission to provide students the information they need on campus dorms to live a happy and healthy college career.
